The MinarTech project is focused on Themes. Each Theme is a Narrative based on Digitized Primary Sources. Thus, Themes are more than a compilations or renderings of these sources as such.

Thematic presentations are constructed by Curators, who are specialists in their respective fields, but Contributions to themes can be submitted to the Curator by anyone with access to primary sources, other material, or information.

Themes are modular. A Module is initially used as a part of one Theme, but they can be reused in the context of other themes. MinarTech projects are Community Driven, but they are not an instance of Social Media. You can initiate a Theme or contribute to an existing one. If you want to submit a material, be in contact with the Curator for the Theme you are interested in. A list of Themes will be provided here, but remember that you can initiate your own Themes.

Your Contribution can be Unverified or Reviewed by experts. If you want to be accepted as a Reviewer, please let us know what your specialities are. To be accepted as a Reviewer for a specific Theme, you are required to have academic credentials and peer reviewed publications of your own.

As many of those writing texts for the service are not writing in their primary language, there is a need for Translation, Language Control and Revision. You are welcome to participate in this role for you own language.

Skills and contributions of Web Developers are welcome, including Designers, Illustrators and Technical Developers.
