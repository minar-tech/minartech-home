Minar Technologies

Leveraging Learning, Teaching and Research
through International Co-operation

MinarTech is an incubator for international, community driven projects aiming at curated, Thematic Services for Cultural Heritage based on digitized primary sources. Such Resources include digitized image collections, digitized manuscripts, Open Access data sets, and Open Source code. We also facilitate supporting projects involving Language Technology, and Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML).

The word "Minar" is common to Arabic, Persian and Hindi. Its original meaning is "place of light", i.e. lighthouse, but in a more generic sense it means "tower". The word "minaret" is one of its acquired meanings. The MinarTech logo symbolizes a tower made up of two triangles. The base of the triangle is consists of the three fundamental tasks of universities: Learning, Teaching and Research. The upper triangle, with Innovation, Culture and Engagement, is supported by the base. Without them, Learning, Teaching and Research have very little, if any, meaning. The two triangles are tied together in the center by international co-operation. Without it the construction collapses.

The outcome of MinarTech Projects will be published in their respective, separate project sites. Prototypes may be published here. Code and Resources of will be shared on GitHub. Co-operation between technical developers and curators will be facilitated using the Discourse forum software.

The MinarTech project and site is in an early phase of development. We follow the principle of Continuous Integration and Development (CI/CD) which means that we are continuously on the move. Join us and find your own role! We work for lasting human value.
